About Shrofile

If you are looking to know professionals beyond their resume - you have come to the right place! If you want to share your personality with the world, yep, you guessed it - You clicked on the right url!

At Shrofile, we help you Share your Profile, With short and easy to create videos that look professional And with social feedback from your friends and colleagues that enhance your personality and make you shine.

Shrofile is a unique video based professional platform that crowdsources and highlights aspects of an individual’s personality. Personality of every individual is showcased through 30 second videos.

With your support, we aim to change the way in which professionals express themselves and interact with each other.

Mission and Vision

Shrofile's Vision

Shrofile aims to change the way in which personalities of professionals are understood by others and the manner in which professionals interact with each other.

1. We see a world where professionals can express themselves more holistically with videos and give answers to probing yet interesting questions such that others get a window into their style, preference and attitude in an engaging way.

2. We foresee our crowd-sourced social analytics to create an unsolicited world view of an individual’s personality in the form of a personality word cloud and a social vibe chart

Shrofile's Mission

Our mission is to - Enable professionals to understand themselves and others better, to nurture an enjoyable and effective community of global workforce
