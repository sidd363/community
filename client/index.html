Why Shrofile?

Professionals - get a chance to build their personal brand by showcasing aspects of their personality thereby differentiating themselves from other.

Organizations - on the other hand can leverage the same platform to showcase their culture, brand and vision to the world by broadcasting videos of their own distinguished management and leadership teams.

Shrofile aims to bring professionals and organizations together like never before, making first time interactions a more efficient and captivating experience. Short 30 second videos allow professionals to view and experience each other's personality beyond Linkedin profiles.
