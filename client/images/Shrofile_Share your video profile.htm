How it works

For Professionals

In today’s world, each one of us is living in a fast paced technological era. And being in a rat race to achieve the best in our careers, it is very important for individuals to build themselves as brands and differentiate themselves from others. Your professional brand is so much more about your choices, preferences and attitudes than where you have been what you have done. In the past, resumes have been the only medium for professionals to express their professional identity and make a first impression in the eyes of employers. Although text is important, it is just not sufficient to describe anyone’s personality.
The movement towards the creation and consumption of video is gargantuan. YouTube has become the third most visited website, the second most popular search engine and the largest video platform on the planet. 75% of executives watch work-related videos at least once a week. ⅓ of all online activity is spent watching videos. 90% of users say that videos are helpful in decision and understanding processes. 96% of B2B organisations use video in some capacity in their marketing campaigns.
So what’s the point that we are trying to make?
We bring the magic of video and the intangibility of personality together. Shrofile gives you the ability of building a video profile that showcases who you really are. Organisations are waking up to realise the importance of the personality of a prospective employee. Business leaders are looking towards building organisations with people who fit into the culture and belief systems of the organisation. Serial entrepreneur Richard Branson believes that “The first thing to look for when searching for a great employee is somebody with a personality that fits with your company culture. Most skills can be learned, but it is difficult to train people on their personality.” Remember the time when you wondered what it was like living the life of an astronaut, or what drove figures like Bill Gates and Nelsen Mandela? Wouldn’t it be great to inspire, educate and empower the leaders of tomorrow? People all over the world want to know more about those that they look up to. You. Conveying your thoughts, experiences and influences in video, to a world of curious eyes enables you to connect at a personal level like never before.
Candidates with sparkling resumes may not have all those qualities as mentioned in their resume while there is no scope of fakes in video resume. However when it comes to video resume, you reflect your real self to your hiring manager and thus it helps you by throwing light on your professional approach towards your job and company. Thus, its 21st century marked by edging technology. Being in rat race, to reach at pinnacle of glory you need to build yourself as a brand. It’s very important on part of an individual to project himself as the best candidate for job and this can be achieved only when you learn to adapt yourself to ever changing world. Video resume is the excellent tool at your disposal that you need to exploit and make it work for you. Technically, through video resume you give reasons to your hiring manager why you are best suited for the job. Initially it may be bit difficult for you, but if you want that job then video resume will surely act as a powerful weapon for you. Go for it!
Not just your work experience and qualification but also the way you stand, you talk and your gestures add up to your personality. A video resume facilitates this insight at an early stage. Whereas in print resume it is tough to have an insight about the personality merely by looking at the printed form of resume.
“First impression is the last impression” and this hold unbeatably true in this case. There are times when you turn out to a bit nervous in personal interviews and fail to show your true self, however, in video resume you can be yourself in your comfort zone. This will give you an edge of impression over those who chose to send a printed or virtual resume.

For Organisations
Companies today continue to grow in size and complexity. It only makes sense that they remain agile in the way they recruit their workforce, where hiring the next generation of business drivers requires new generation methodology and techniques.
Statistics tell us that it takes an individual only a few seconds to gather a first impression of a candidate. With interviews lasting from 30 - 60 mins, interviewers end up spending a lot of time reinforcing their initial thoughts. What if there was a more efficient ways to sift through candidates and know what they are like outside of their CV’s before committing the time to interview them? Shrofile gives you, the employer, the opportunity to optimise your recruitment process. Enabling and encouraging applicant to create and build their personal video profiles provides an inside view of the applicant, allowing you to meet them without having to physically meet them. You get to know so much more about the candidate without having to devote a significant amount of time and energy.
